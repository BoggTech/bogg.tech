My Projects

Here's a quick overview of a selection of projects I've worked on over the years. This list is not exhaustive. A number of links may be unavailable, but I'm working to get those repositories public.


College

SecureBox - 2025

An example program for encrypting files with symmetric and asymmetric encryption methods. Developed solo as part of CSU33032's "Securing the Cloud" assignment. Files are stored in a shared box account, but users of the software can upload and read encrypted files.


MoveWise - 2025

A simple journey planner app developed with Python & Expo. Created in a group as part of CSU33013. The role of third years in the team was more "managerial" as part of the module, so I did not touch as much of the code as second year members of the team.


GameBase - 2024

Simple review platform for video games, powered by the IGDB API. Developed in a group as part of CSU33012.


FlightVis - 2023

An application made in Processing for processing flight data from a CSV. Developed in a group as part of CSU11013.


Personal

VisorView - 2024

A small tool I developed to take transparent screenshots and view the animations of Toontown Rewritten characters. Developed in Python with Panda3D.


Make-A-Toon - 2021

A Twitter bot that generates random Toontown Rewritten Toons, renders them, and posts them to Twitter. Developed in Python with Panda3D.
